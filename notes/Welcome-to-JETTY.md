# Welcome to JETTY™

JETTY™ is named for the Spiral Jetty — Robert Smithson's 1970 earthwork on the north shore of the Great Salt Lake. A coil of black basalt rocks spiraling into hypersaline pink-red water. Every idea you save here becomes a star in your galaxy, connected to every other star that shares something with it.

You can talk to JETTY™ out loud or type. Say "remember that" followed by anything — a thought, a decision, a name — and it becomes a permanent star in your galaxy. Ask any question and JETTY™ answers from what you've saved.

JETTY™ runs on your server. Your data is yours. No AI company owns your thinking.
