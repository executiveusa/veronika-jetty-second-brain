# Silicon Slopes — Salt Lake City Tech Scene 2026

Silicon Slopes is Utah's tech corridor running through Salt Lake City, Lehi, and Provo. Major anchors include Adobe, Qualtrics, Domo, Podium, HireVue, and thousands of startups.

Salt Lake City posts 17.7 new AI jobs per 100,000 residents — 50 percent above the national average. Governor Spencer Cox launched a 100-million dollar Pro-Human AI initiative.

Top employers 2026: Adobe, Qualtrics, Podium (W16), HireVue, PassiveLogic, Sameday (W23), Corgi Insurance (S24). Key VC: Sorenson Capital, Signal Peak Ventures, Album VC.

Networking: Silicon Slopes Summit (annual), 801 Labs (workshops), Women in Tech SLC.
