# How to Export Your AI Data

Your conversation history belongs to you. Here is how to get it.

ChatGPT: Settings → Data Controls → Export Data → check email → download zip (JSON files).

Claude.ai: Settings → Privacy → Export your data → downloads immediately.

Google Gemini: myaccount.google.com → Data and Privacy → Google Takeout → select Gemini Apps Activity.

Perplexity: Settings → Privacy → Export Data.

After export: Drop files into JETTY™'s notes folder. Your entire AI history becomes searchable in your galaxy. Your thinking stays yours.
