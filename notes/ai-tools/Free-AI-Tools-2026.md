# Free AI Tools — The Honest Guide

Groq: Fastest free AI. Uses Llama 3.3 70B. Best for quick questions and drafts. Free at groq.com. JETTY™ default free model.

Perplexity Free: Best for research with citations. Every answer has verifiable sources. Use for company research before interviews.

Google Gemini Free: Connected to Google Search. Best for current events and recent information.

Mistral Free: Privacy-first, European. Good writing and analysis. Free tier at console.mistral.ai.

Claude.ai Free: Strong reasoning and long documents. Use for complex tasks and reading contracts.

Rule of thumb: Groq for speed, Perplexity for research, Claude for complexity. JETTY™ switches between all of these on command.
