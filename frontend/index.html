What Jetty is

Not a chatbot. A deployable memory system that runs on your server.

Every file you drop in, every note you speak, every idea you capture becomes a searchable star in your private knowledge galaxy. Jetty connects the dots you don't have time to connect yourself.

01

Upload

Drop in ChatGPT exports, Gemini notes, a résumé, client documents, or plain text.

02

Jetty extracts useful facts and connects them to goals, people, projects, and dates.

03

Act

It drafts emails, job-search plans, follow-ups, and private next steps in plain language.

How it works

Signal. Shape. Action.

You bring the raw material. Jetty brings the structure. Every interaction makes the system smarter about your specific world.

Signal → upload, paste, speak, capture

Shape → summarize, connect, tag, cite

Action → plan, write, follow up, remember

Privacy and control

Your data lives on your server. Not ours.

Jetty runs on your own infrastructure. No SaaS account owns your thinking. Export your history from ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or Perplexity and bring it home — permanently.

Private.

Your vault is encrypted, self-hosted, and never shared with any AI provider. Switch models without losing your memory.
