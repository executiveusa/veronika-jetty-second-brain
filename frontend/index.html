What Jetty is

Not a chatbot. A private memory studio that lives with you.

Every file you drop in, every note you speak, every idea you capture becomes a searchable star in your private knowledge galaxy. Jetty connects the dots you do not have time to connect yourself.

How it works

Signal. Shape. Action.

You bring the raw material. Jetty brings the structure. Every interaction makes the system smarter about your specific world.

Signal → upload, paste, speak, capture

Shape → summarize, connect, tag, cite

Action → plan, write, follow up, remember

Privacy and control

Your data stays with you. Not in a generic SaaS cloud.

Jetty runs on your own infrastructure. No platform owns your thinking. Export your history from ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or Perplexity and bring it home permanently.

Private.

Your vault is encrypted, self-hosted, and never shared with any AI provider. Switch models without losing your memory.
